Offerings

All sessions currently offered online. In-person work in Playa del Carmen available upon request. Retreat inquiries welcome.

Private Sessions

One-on-one sessions that hold space for what matters.

This isn’t coaching. This isn’t talk therapy either.

It’s the kind of conversation most people never get to have—
a quiet space to meet yourself honestly,
and come back to what matters.

We meet weekly, biweekly, or monthly—you set the pace.

You’re in the middle of a transition, and don’t know what it means yet You’re holding things that don’t belong to you, but can’t put them down You’re ready for something real—without knowing what it is

Relationship Work

Because sometimes the mirror needs a mirror.

Whether you come as a couple, friends, or two people sorting the space between you—this is not about fixing what’s broken. It’s about meeting what’s true.

We’ll work with what’s spoken, unspoken, and felt between you.

You’re navigating rupture, resentment, or return You want to stay—but not in the same shape You’re ready to listen differently

Retreats

Immersive space for reentry, reweaving, and rest.

I offer private and small group retreats in Playa del Carmen. These are not programs. They’re designed moments of slowing down, deepening, and remembering.

Some retreats are bespoke—designed with you. Others are seasonal and thematic.

You feel the need to step out of your world, but not away from yourself You’ve been carrying too much for too long You want a space that doesn’t ask for performance—just presence
