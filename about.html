About

This isn’t new for me.

I was reading Buddhist texts before I understood half the words. Trying to meditate before I even knew what I was really seeking.

I trained as a therapist—but what I offer isn’t just therapy. It’s the braid I’ve spent my life living:

  • Grief and myth.
  • Pop music and prayer.
  • Gestalt and God.
  • Sexuality and sacredness, held in the same palm.

We might talk about your breakup. Your workplace. Your childhood. But underneath, we’ll be touching something older:

  • “Why am I here?”
  • “What is this moment trying to teach me?”
  • “What have I been carrying that was never mine?”

I don’t offer answers. But I do offer a space where you can ask your real questions—the ones you’ve been circling without words.

Moin Subhani, Registered Psychotherapist (RP), Ontario
Practicing online and from Playa del Carmen
